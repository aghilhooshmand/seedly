# Seedly — Product Vision

## The idea

Seedly helps people **plant intentions and grow them to completion**. It started as a tool to support a job search — tracking applications, interviews, and progress toward a target role — and generalizes to **any meaningful goal in life**: learning a skill, moving house, training for a race, building a side project.

Most todo apps list tasks. Seedly tracks the **full lifecycle** of a goal: from first idea through stages, milestones, setbacks, and finally reaching the target.

## Who it's for

- Individuals pursuing long-running personal goals (job search, education, health)
- Couples or families who want a shared, calm view of progress
- Anyone who prefers **journey tracking** over checkbox anxiety

## Core principles

1. **Lifecycle, not just tasks** — Every goal moves through defined stages (e.g. *Prepare → Apply → Interview → Offer*).
2. **Gentle accountability** — Progress is visible without guilt; pausing is valid.
3. **One place for the story** — Notes, activities, and milestones live with the goal.
4. **Simple and warm** — Clean UI, low friction, works on phone and desktop.

## MVP scope (v0.1)

- Create goals with a life area (Career, Health, Learning, Personal, Other)
- Define lifecycle stages per goal (templates for job search included)
- Log activities and notes on a timeline
- Dashboard showing active goals and recent progress
- Local SQLite database; PostgreSQL path documented for production

## Out of scope (for now)

- Multi-user auth and sharing
- Email reminders and calendar sync
- Mobile native apps
- AI suggestions

## Success metrics (personal)

- Wife uses it weekly during job search
- Can see at a glance: *where am I, what's next, how far to target*
- Codebase is documented enough that future-you can extend it confidently
